About Randomization
Are the typical numbers you generate from a random number generator actually random? Truthfully, they are not. They are what is known as psuedo-random. Psuedo-random numbers are generated using mathematical formulas to appear as if they are random numbers but actually not random. So Randomization offers you true random numbers based on Random.Org's service which generates random numbers from atmostpheric noise. For the time being this application requires an Internet connection. In future releases this application will allow you to switch between psuedo-random and true random numbers.
